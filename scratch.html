Why I Love The Beatles

  1. Personal musical influence
    • They inspired me to learn guitar
    • Inspired me to start a band with my best friends in High School
  2. They changed music forever
    • Invented new recording techniques
    • Blended genres in ways never done before
    • Created fusions of rock and psychedelia
  3. Timeless songwriting
    • Catchy, emotional, and deep lyrics
    • From "Something" to "Yesterday" to "Let it Be"
    • Their writing and musicality was unlike anything ever made
